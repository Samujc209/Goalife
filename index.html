Install Goalife
On Android (Chrome):
1️⃣ Tap the ⋮ menu (top right)
2️⃣ Tap "Add to Home screen"
3️⃣ Tap "Add"
On iPhone / iPad (Safari):
1️⃣ Tap the ⎙ Share button (bottom bar)
2️⃣ Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3️⃣ Tap "Add"
⚠️ Must be opened in Safari, not Chrome
On Desktop (Chrome):
1️⃣ Look for the install icon ⊕ in the address bar
2️⃣ Click it and select "Install"
Works offline • No app store needed • Free forever
